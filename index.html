What We Offer

From learning basic and in-demand digital skills to finding freelance opportunities from SMEs, Start-ups, and NGOs, Telos got you covered!
Telos is an online learning platform that focuses on reskilling and upskilling individuals to strengthen the digital workforce of the Philippines while providing opportunities that will. This is made possible by the platform's extensive dossier of courses, quality modules designed by experts, a combination of AI and human mentors, and a strong reporting and monitoring system to ensure learning.
Through emotional analytics and with the help of our chatbot, Telos highlights the importance of mental wellness and addresses the isolation and lack of interaction experienced by students completing the courses on their own.

Self Paced Courses

Learn at a pace that best suits your learning time and schedule. Balance work, life, and studies by taking our self-paced modules guided by your AI companion that anticipates your questions and needs.

Time Bound Courses

Nostalgic for a classroom environment? Join our time bound group courses! Interact with a teacher, connect with your classmates, and complete a course within schedule.

Certified Coaches

We choose the best for you. Our mentors are experts in their respective fields and have designed modules that would fit the learning goals of individuals in different levels.

Emotional Analytics

This feature aims to improve your learning experience through an optional collection of verbal and non-verbal communication cues which will allow us to further personalize your experience while you are with Telos.

Freelance Job Oppurtunity

Apply your newly acquired skills by taking on freelance opportunities posted by our partner SMEs, Start-Ups, and NGOs. These freelance jobs and projects can be a new source of additional income or a shift to a non-traditional and more flexible career.

Corporate Training

We understand the importance of equipping your team with relevant digital skills. Telos works with partner companies in developing a workforce that is both competent and future-ready.

Telos Ecosystem

Recognizing the need to further empower the Filipino workforce with in-demand digital skills, the team designed Telos to be an online learning platform that empowers offers courses designed by expert mentors to accommodate individuals on different levels of learning. In these trying times, we are able to provide real world experience to the members of our community while giving them the opportunity to have an additional source incoming. This is made possible by our industry partners who shares the same advocacy as Telos, empowering Filipinos through education and equipping them with the skills needed in order for them to become future ready leaders. Taking our commitment to this advocacy a notch higher, we even utilized artificial intelligence to provide our members a companion in learning and use emotional analytics to further personalize their experience.
